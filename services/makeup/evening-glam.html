Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my evening glam appointment?

For special events and occasions, we recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons like holidays and wedding months. For last-minute bookings, we do maintain a waitlist and occasionally have cancellations, so don't hesitate to contact us even if your event is soon.

For regular clients attending multiple events, we offer a subscription service with priority booking and discounted rates. This ensures you always have access to our artists when you need them most.

What should I bring to my appointment?

We recommend bringing inspiration photos, your outfit (or fabric swatch), any special jewelry you'll be wearing, and your preferred lip color if you have one. While we have an extensive professional kit, if you have sensitive skin or specific product preferences, feel free to bring your favorite foundation or other products.

For bridal clients, we suggest having your hair trial completed before your makeup trial so we can see the complete look. Also consider bringing photos of your venue as lighting conditions can influence our approach.

How do I maintain my makeup throughout the evening?

We provide all clients with a custom touch-up kit containing blotting papers, a mini setting spray, lip color, and a small brush for powder application. The key to longevity is minimal touching of your face, blotting rather than rubbing when needed, and strategic powder application only on areas that get oily.

For particularly long events, we offer an "event support" package where an artist remains on-site for touch-ups. We also teach you quick fixes for common issues like smudged liner or faded lip color during your appointment.
