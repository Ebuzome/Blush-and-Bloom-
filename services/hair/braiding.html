Artistry Woven into Every Strand

Our master braiders approach their craft with the reverence it deserves. With hands trained in both traditional techniques and contemporary innovations, they transform hair into living sculpture. We work with premium, ethically-sourced materials that honor both your hair's integrity and our planet's wellbeing.

When you sit in our chair, you're not just getting a hairstyleâ€”you're becoming part of a legacy. Crown Weavers is a sanctuary where ancestral wisdom meets modern luxury, where each creation is a testament to the beauty and resilience of our heritage.

The art of braiding dates back thousands of years, with each culture developing its own unique styles and techniques. From the intricate cornrows of West Africa to the elaborate updos of ancient Egypt, braids have always been more than just a way to style hairâ€”they've been a means of communication, a symbol of status, and a celebration of identity.

Today, we continue this tradition by blending time-honored techniques with modern innovations. Our braiders are trained in over 50 different braiding styles from across the African diaspora, ensuring that we can create the perfect look for every client, whether you're looking for something traditional or contemporary.

We use only the highest quality, ethically-sourced human hair and extensions, and all of our products are carefully selected to nourish and protect your hair. Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our braiding services

How long do braids typically last?

With proper care, our braids typically last 6-8 weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and recommend using our specially formulated braid preservation products to extend the life of your style. Factors like your hair type, the style you choose, and your maintenance routine can all affect longevity. We recommend scheduling a touch-up appointment at the 4-week mark for styles like feed-in braids or updos.

Can I request a custom style not shown here?

Absolutely! Our master braiders specialize in creating custom designs tailored to your vision. During your consultation, bring inspiration photos or describe your ideal look, and we'll assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to create something uniquely yours. We've brought to life everything from braided interpretations of client's favorite artworks to styles incorporating meaningful cultural symbols.

What should I do to prepare for my braiding appointment?

For best results: 1) Arrive with clean, dry hair (shampooed within 24 hours) 2) Avoid heavy products that cause buildup 3) Eat a good meal beforehand as some styles take several hours 4) Bring inspiration photos 5) Wear comfortable clothing with a button-down top 6) For long sessions, consider bringing headphones or a book. We provide neck pillows and refreshments to keep you comfortable.

Do you provide hair or should I bring my own?

We offer premium, ethically-sourced hair extensions in various textures and colors that have been specially selected for quality and performance. However, you're welcome to bring your own hair if you prefer. During your consultation, we'll advise you on the best type and amount of hair needed for your chosen style. All hair purchases from Crown Weavers come with a complimentary deep conditioning treatment.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations (within 24 hours) will incur a 50% fee of the scheduled service cost, as braiding appointments often take multiple hours we could have allocated to other clients. In cases of emergency, please contact us as soon as possible. We appreciate your understanding as this policy helps us respect both our stylists' time and our clients' access to appointments.

How do I care for my braids between appointments?

Our comprehensive aftercare includes: 1) Using a silk/satin scarf or pillowcase 2) Applying our braid spray every 2-3 days to moisturize 3) Cleansing your scalp weekly with a diluted shampoo 4) Avoiding heavy products that cause buildup 5) Protecting edges with silk wraps during exercise 6) Scheduling a professional take-down to prevent damage. We provide detailed written instructions and video tutorials for each specific style.

Are your braiding techniques suitable for all hair types?

Yes! Our stylists are trained in specialized techniques for every hair type - from fine, straight hair to coily textures. During your consultation, we assess your hair's density, elasticity, and condition to determine the best braiding method and tension level. We offer protective alternatives like crochet braids for those with fragile hair or sensitive scalps. Your hair's health is always our top priority.
