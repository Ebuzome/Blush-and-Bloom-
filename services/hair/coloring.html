Top 5 Hair Coloring Tips

Maintain your vibrant color with our expert advice

Moisturize Regularly

Colored hair needs extra hydration. Use sulfate-free shampoos and deep conditioning treatments weekly to maintain moisture balance and prevent fading.

Protect from UV Rays

Sun exposure can cause color to fade. Use UV-protectant sprays and wear hats when spending extended time outdoors to preserve your color's vibrancy.

Wash Less Frequently

Limit washing to 2-3 times per week with cool water. This helps maintain your color's intensity and prevents premature fading.

Use Color-Safe Products

Invest in professional color-safe shampoos and conditioners. These products are pH-balanced to protect your color and prevent brassiness.

Schedule Regular Touch-Ups

Maintain your color with regular salon visits. Touch-ups every 4-6 weeks keep your roots fresh and your color looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coloring services

Is hair coloring safe for natural hair?

Absolutely! At Crown Weavers, we use premium, ammonia-free color products that are gentle on your hair while delivering vibrant results. Our colorists are trained to assess your hair's condition and use techniques that minimize damage. We always begin with a strand test and recommend treatments to maintain your hair's health throughout the coloring process.

How long will the color last?

Our professional color typically lasts 6-8 weeks with proper care. Vibrant fashion colors may fade slightly faster, while traditional shades maintain their richness longer. We provide personalized aftercare instructions and recommend touch-ups every 4-6 weeks to maintain optimal color. Factors like hair porosity, washing frequency, and sun exposure can affect longevity.

Can I customize my own color blend?

Yes! Our master colorists specialize in creating custom color blends tailored to your unique vision. During your consultation, we'll discuss your desired outcome, assess your skin tone, and create a personalized formula. We can blend multiple shades to achieve everything from subtle dimension to bold, head-turning color transformations.
