Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our styling services

How long does a styling session take?

Styling sessions typically range from 45 minutes to 2 hours depending on the complexity of the style. Simple touch-ups may take as little as 30 minutes, while intricate updos for special events can take up to 2.5 hours. We recommend allowing extra time for your consultation to ensure we fully understand your vision. During your booking, we'll provide a time estimate based on your selected style.

Should I come with freshly washed hair?

For best results, we recommend washing your hair 24-48 hours before your appointment. Slightly textured hair holds styles better than freshly washed hair. Please arrive with dry, detangled hair without heavy products. If your hair is freshly washed, let us know so we can apply texturizing products to help the style hold. Avoid using heavy conditioners or leave-in products that might make your hair too slippery.

Do you offer trial styles for brides or events?

Yes! We highly recommend trial sessions for brides and anyone preparing for a major event. During your trial, we'll work with you to create your perfect look, take photos from all angles, and make any necessary adjustments. Trials typically last 1.5-2 hours and include a detailed consultation. We'll note exactly how we created your style so we can replicate it perfectly on your special day.
